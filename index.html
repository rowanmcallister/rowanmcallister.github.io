Biography

I am a machine learning scientist at the Toyota Research Institute in California focusing on autonomous vehicle planning. Previously I was a postdoc in the Robotic AI and Learning Lab with Sergey Levine, working on probabilistic modeling toward data-efficient reinforcement learning and autonomous vehicle planning.
