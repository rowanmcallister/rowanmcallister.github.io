Biography

I am a machine learning manager at the Toyota Research Institute in California focusing on autonomous vehicle planning. Previously, I was a PhD student at the Machine Learning Group with Carl Rasmussen and a postdoc in the Robotic AI and Learning Lab with Sergey Levine, working on probabilistic modeling toward data-efficient reinforcement learning and autonomous vehicle planning.
